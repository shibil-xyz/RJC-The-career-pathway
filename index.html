class="yellow">ABOUT US

At RJC - The Career Pathway, we are a team of first-year engineering students from IES College of Engineering, dedicated to helping fellow engineering students kickstart their careers. We provide essential study materials and placement resources that freshers need to succeed. Whether you're navigating the complexities of academics or preparing for campus placements, we’re here to support your journey. Our mission is to simplify the process for students by offering curated content that covers study materials, interview preparation, and more, ensuring that you’re fully equipped for both exams and your future career. We aim to be the go-to platform for all things related to engineering education and placements. Founded by Harinandan K, alongside Shibil CP as co-founder, and Dheeraj Prasad as our market analyst, our team is committed to understanding the unique needs of students and providing relevant and up-to-date resources. We recognize the challenges freshers face and are constantly evolving to meet the demands of today’s engineering students. Join us as we create a space where students can focus on what matters most—learning and preparing for their careers.
